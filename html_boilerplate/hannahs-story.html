Blood of a Royal

Chapter 1

The cold marble flooring of the Greaat Hall was uncomfortable below Elvira's right knee as she knelt before her King's throne. She gripped the handle of the ceremonial sword in her hands, pressing the lip into the dark blue cushion placed in front of her. The King was yet to make an appearance and Elvira took the opportunity to study the sword she was so carefully holding; she had seen it before but never so close. The hilt was a deep red in colour, whith the pommel decorated with blood rubies, mined from the Kingdom of Tamaria. The blade was carved with intricate details; telling the story of the first King of Pallau slaying the great Wyvern, Fenlake the Red, which threw the newly founded country into a great boom, benefitting al. Elvira knew the sword she held in her hands was over seven hundred years old and was only brought out for knighting ceremonies like this.
